显示值 使用值
@html{d.screenName} @html{d.value}

可能的格式包括

  1. [screennamefile:valuefield]\@{binding} -->从计算的列表中通过给定的字段名获取值和名称
  2. {namevalue1,namevalue2,namevaluen} -->从给的值列表中获取
  3. {name1:value1,name2:value2,namen:valuen} -->从给定的值列表获取
  4. [screennamefiled:valuefield]/XEnum/Path -->从X枚举路径获取
  5. [namefieldname:valuefieldname]schema:/path/to/schema:sql
  6. [namefieldname:valuefieldname]schema:/path/to/schema:/*nocache*/sql
  7. sql:/path/to/schema:fullsql
  8. sql:/path/to/schema:/*nocache*/full sql
  9. [screennamefile:valuefield]com.mapbased.prj.EnumClassName
